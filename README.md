## Key Projects

### 1. Founder Profiling & Investment Evaluation

Analyzed founder-background and business-idea fit for a fintech remittance startup. Evaluated investor value creation beyond capital, focusing on credibility, regulatory trust, strategic partnerships, and market positioning.

**Key Concepts:**
- Founder Profiling
- Startup Evaluation
- Investor Value Creation
- Market Positioning
- Strategic Partnerships

---

### 2. Augmented Reality EdTech Investment Analysis

Conducted startup-stage assessment, SWOT analysis, Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), Customer Lifetime Value (CLV), and convertible note structuring for an AR-based education technology venture.

**Key Concepts:**
- SWOT Analysis
- CAC & CLV Modelling
- Startup Valuation
- Convertible Notes
- Investment Milestones

---

### 3. Private Equity Fund Setup

Designed a climate-technology-focused private equity fund, including:

- Investment thesis
- Target sectors
- HNI and Family Office investor selection
- Company scouting strategy
- Investment screening framework

**Key Concepts:**
- Fund Formation
- Investment Thesis Development
- Portfolio Strategy
- Deal Sourcing
- Screening Parameters

---

### 4. Private Equity Exit Strategy Analysis

Evaluated Blackstone's exit from Embassy Office Parks REIT through IPO and public-market transactions. Analyzed value creation, exit timing, return generation, and private equity exit strategies.

**Key Concepts:**
- IPO Exit Strategy
- Value Creation
- CAGR Estimation
- Portfolio Exit Planning
- Public Market Liquidity
